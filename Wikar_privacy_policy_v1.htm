Wikar Privacy Policy v1

Wikar

Wikar does not currently log, record, or transmit any user data, independent of the services listed below.

In addition to ARCore, we use your camera to scan QR codes. Scanning and processing happens completely on the device, using a library called zxing.

Clowder

The Clowder web server which hosts downloadable content has its own terms of service. By using the app, we assume you accept these terms. Downloaded content from Clowder is not stored permanently on the device.

Clowder Policies and Terms: https://cyprus.ncsa.illinois.edu/clowder/tos

Google ARCore

Wikar is built using Unity's native ARFoundation to provide positional tracking through your devices internal sensors, including the camera. On android, this uses Google Play Services for AR (ARCore), provided by Google LLC and governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

ARCore: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.ar.core

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

ARCore is a seperate service downloaded on your phone. We assume you accept its terms of use and privacy policy independent of our app.

Unity

Unity collects information about your device to make the software run correctly for it. We use raw sensor data as well as the camera to perform our tracking and scanning. By using this software, we assume you accept Unity's terms and privacy policy. We do not use Unity's Unity’s Analytics or Ad services.

Read about Unity's privacy policy here: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy
